Beef Recipe

Description

A beef dish is a savory and hearty meal featuring succulent pieces of beef cooked with flavorful ingredients to create a delicious and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • Beef (cubed or sliced)
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products (sauce, diced tomatoes, or paste)
  • Spices and seasonings (such as salt, pepper, thyme, rosemary)
  • Vegetables (carrots, potatoes, or your choice)
  • Beef broth or stock
  • Oil or butter for cooking
  • Flour (optional for thickening)

Steps

  1. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add beef to the pan and brown on all sides.
  4. Stir in tomato products and spices, ensuring the beef is well-coated.
  5. If using flour for thickening, sprinkle it over the mixture and stir until combined.
  6. Pour in beef broth or stock to cover the beef, and bring to a simmer.
  7. Add vegetables and continue to simmer until the beef is tender and vegetables are cooked.
  8. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve hot.

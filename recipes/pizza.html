Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a delicious and versatile dish consisting of a thin or thick crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarella, parmesan, or your favorite)
  • Various toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Herbs and spices (oregano, basil, red pepper flakes, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven according to the pizza dough instructions.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings, distributing them evenly.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and, if desired, sprinkle minced garlic on top.
  7. Season with herbs and spices for extra flavor.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
